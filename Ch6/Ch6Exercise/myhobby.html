Why I Love Volleyball

Volleyball keeps me active, focused, and surrounded by great teammates. I play weekly and try to improve a little each time.

I enjoy the strategy and communication that volleyball requires. Whether I’m practicing serves or working on defensive positioning, there’s always something to refine. The energy of a close match is the best motivation to keep training.

If you want to learn more about the sport, the resources at USA Volleyball are a great starting point for players and fans.
