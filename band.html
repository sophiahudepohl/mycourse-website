Favorite Releases (description list)

Rumours (1977)
Front-to-back classics and airtight songwriting. “Dreams,” “Go Your Own Way,” and “The Chain” showcase harmonies, tension, and immaculate production—still a gold standard for pop-rock albums.
Tusk (1979)
Adventurous and a little wild—raw edges, experimental textures, and big hooks. It balances Lindsey’s eccentric ideas with Stevie and Christine’s melodic heart.
Tango in the Night (1987)
Glossy, shimmering ‘80s perfection. “Everywhere,” “Little Lies,” and “Big Love” pair layered production with instantly memorable melodies.
