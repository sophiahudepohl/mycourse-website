Focus on Web Design #1 — Site Review

Hands-On Exercise #2 blog post

Website Chosen

a) URL: https://www.airbnb.com

Purpose & Audience

b) Purpose: Help people find and book short-term stays and experiences, and let hosts list properties.

c) Intended audience: Travelers looking for places to stay, and potential hosts/property owners.

Effectiveness

d) Does it reach its audience? Yes. The homepage centers a simple search (location/dates/guests), uses strong imagery, clear categories, and keeps “Become a host” visible—meeting both traveler and host needs.

Usefulness to Me

e) Useful for comparing options quickly with filters (price, beds, amenities) and a live map that updates as you move it.

Appeal (Design & UX)

f) Clean layout, generous white space, consistent iconography, accessible color contrast, and predictable navigation. Some pop-up modals feel busy on smaller screens, but overall it’s polished.

Recommend?

g) Yes—inventory is broad and the interface is easy to learn. It’s also good for inspiration even if I book elsewhere.

Improvements

h) Show total price (with fees/taxes) earlier in results, simplify a few mobile filters, and surface host verification/safety indicators more clearly.
